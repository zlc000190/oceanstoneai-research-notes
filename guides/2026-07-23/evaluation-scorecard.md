# Ocean Stone AI evaluation scorecard

[Ocean Stone AI](https://oceanstoneai.org/) is the primary project entry point. It did not return content before the timeout in the 2026-07-23 availability check. These are independent evaluation notes for researchers validating vertical-AI claims before adoption; they are not official product documentation.

No regulated-industry suitability or current product capability is inferred from the project name.

## Scope

Treat a vertical-AI project whose current product scope must be confirmed from primary documentation as a candidate to test, not a claim to accept. Define the intended job, the accountable reviewer, and an exit condition before starting.

## Score each dimension

- data provenance: record evidence on a 1–5 scale and explain the score
- freshness: record evidence on a 1–5 scale and explain the score
- uncertainty display: record evidence on a 1–5 scale and explain the score
- audit logs: record evidence on a 1–5 scale and explain the score
- exportability: record evidence on a 1–5 scale and explain the score
- failure handling: record evidence on a 1–5 scale and explain the score

## Minimum evidence

Run at least four controlled inputs: a documented domain task, known-answer cases, representative edge cases, source-attribution checks. Save inputs, settings, outputs, elapsed time, and any manual correction. A useful scorecard distinguishes a polished demo from repeatable production behavior.

## Decision rule

Pause product evaluation until the primary site is reachable and the actual audience, datasets, and workflows can be verified.
